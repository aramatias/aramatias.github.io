About Me

Hey there, Welcome to my creative corner!


I am Arabela Mae Matias,

I am Arabela Mae Matias, a 21-year-old third-year BSIT student at Caraga State University. While my academic journey is grounded in the world of Information Technology, my heart finds its true rhythm in the realm of creativity and leadership.

A Graphic Designer, Artist, Photographer, and Student Leader by title, I am on a quest to bridge the digital and artistic worlds. Through the lens of my camera and the strokes of my designs, I capture the essence of moments and translate them into vibrant narratives. With each project, I weave a unique tapestry of emotions and experiences.

My creative journey is a kaleidoscope of colors, where pixels and paint merge into something extraordinary. It's not just about creating visuals; it's about telling stories that resonate with the soul. Every click of the camera and every brushstroke on the canvas is an opportunity to immerse myself in the depth of human expression.

Join me as I unfold the chapters of my artistic odyssey and explore the vibrant tapestry of my passions and pursuits. Let's embark on this adventure together, where art and technology coexist, and where the intersection of innovation and imagination knows no bounds.
